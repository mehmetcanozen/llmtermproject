The project proposal should be one paragraph (200–400 words).

Your proposal should describe:

the problem you will investigate and why it is interesting

the readings you will examine to provide context and background

the data you will use, and if you are collecting new data, how you will collect it

the method or algorithm you are proposing

whether you will use existing implementations, how you will use them, and how you plan to improve or modify them

your general approach to solving the problem, even if you do not yet have an exact answer

how you will evaluate your results

the kind of qualitative results you expect, such as plots or figures

the quantitative analysis you will use to evaluate and/or compare your results, such as performance metrics or statistical tests

if the project is combined with another course or research project, the unique portion that will count toward this class

Submission: Please submit your proposal as a PDF.
